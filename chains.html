Chains Scanner (TCS) — What It Checks

  • New-Pair/Mint Radar: Watches factory events, mempools, and indexers for day-zero listings.
  • Liquidity & Locks: LP size, lock proofs, unlock schedules, creator balances, and dump paths.
  • Ownership & Taxes: Renounce status, max wallet/tx, buy/sell taxes, blacklist patterns.
  • Honeypot/Trading Sim: Static + runtime checks with small-value test calls before entry.
  • Off-Chain Signals: Repo age, contract diffs, social entropy, copy-paste heuristics.

TCS fuses these features into a human-readable verdict (score + reasons) and streams it to dashboards.

Luna — Blockchain Mode

In blockchain mode, Luna connects to TCS and Chainpad with an opt-in wallet, policy engine, and full observability. She can learn from outcomes, apply guardrails, and (if enabled and funded) execute entries/exits.

How Luna uses TCS

  1. Ingest: Luna subscribes to TCS verdicts (score + reasons) for new tokens.
  2. Policy: Your thresholds, allow/deny lists, cooldowns, and quotas are applied.
  3. Act (optional): If trading is enabled and Luna’s wallet is funded, she can place a test buy, monitor slippage/taxes, and manage take-profit/stop—otherwise she stays read-only.
  4. Learn: PnL/behavior feeds back to improve Luna’s ranking and timing.
