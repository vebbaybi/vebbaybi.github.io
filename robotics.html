Projects

ACEBOT — Autonomous Cognitive Evo

Voice-aware patrol bot on Arduino Uno R4: PIR + dual ultrasonic, threat scoring, pan-servo tracking, LED/buzzer feedback, and optional ESP32-CAM relay. Built with PlatformIO and a clean module split.

  • PIR
  • Ultrasonic×2
  • Servo
  • ESP32-CAM

X-EYE — Motion Detection & Telemetry

PIR + ultrasonic fusion with MPU6050 orientation (pitch/roll/yaw), servo scanning, SD/serial logging, and a Python dashboard (Tkinter + Matplotlib). Modes: console, dashboard, hybrid.

  • MPU6050
  • Telemetry
  • SD Logs
  • GUI

VIGI AI Cam — Smart Surveillance

ESP32-CAM surveillance tie-in with PIR and dual ultrasonic triangulation, servo targeting, threat scoring, IR/Bluetooth control modes, and OTA-style command relays. Lives here and surfaces on AI as vision/edge.

  • ESP32-CAM
  • PIR
  • HC-SR04×2
  • Servo

Architecture Overview

Sensors & Fusion

PIR, ultrasonic, and IMU streams normalized and fused with sanity checks and debouncing.

State & Control

Patrol → Tracking → Alert, with threat scores, cooldowns, and watchdogs for recovery.

Telemetry & Logs

Serial/BT dashboards, JSON logs with rotation, correlation IDs, and SD fallbacks.
